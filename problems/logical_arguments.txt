# Logical Arguments for Analysis

Analyze each argument below. For each one:
1. Classify as VALID or INVALID
2. Identify any logical fallacies present
3. Explain your reasoning step-by-step

---

## Argument 1

All mammals have hearts.
All dogs have hearts.
Therefore, all dogs are mammals.

---

## Argument 2

If it rains, the ground will be wet.
The ground is wet.
Therefore, it rained.

---

## Argument 3

All birds can fly.
Penguins are birds.
Therefore, penguins can fly.

---

## Argument 4

Some philosophers are wealthy.
Some wealthy people are politicians.
Therefore, some philosophers are politicians.

---

## Argument 5

If a number is even, then it is divisible by 2.
The number 6 is divisible by 2.
Therefore, 6 is even.

---

## Argument 6

All squares are rectangles.
All rectangles have four sides.
Therefore, all squares have four sides.

---

## Argument 7

The more you study, the better you do on exams.
John studies more than anyone else.
Therefore, John will do the best on the exam.

---

## Argument 8

All cats are mammals.
No reptiles are mammals.
Therefore, no cats are reptiles.

---

## Argument 9

If you work hard, you will succeed.
Alice succeeded.
Therefore, Alice worked hard.

---

## Argument 10

All politicians are liars.
My brother is a politician.
Therefore, my brother is a liar.
